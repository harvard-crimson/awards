The Harvard Crimson's Journalism Awards program will recognize outstanding student work from a variety of different categories. Some options include the ability to enter as an individual or as a school. All award categories will feature multiple ranks of award winners.

  • Categories
Best Overall Newspaper Consistently demonstrate outstanding quality of writing, reporting, design, photography, and breadth of coverage.
Best Newspaper Design Consistently demonstrate outstanding quality of professional and creative layout design.
Best Website Consistently demonstrate outstanding quality of website design and infrastructure; make use of Internet-capabilities to present news in creative and innovative ways.
Reporter of the Year Consistently demonstrate outstanding quality of writing and reporting and show sensitivity to issues where necessary.
Best News Article Demonstrate outstanding quality of writing and reporting and the relevancy of the issue; comprehensively inform the reader.
Best Feature/In-Depth Demonstrate outstanding quality of writing, reporting, and organization of ideas; present a multi-faceted story with nuance and clearly link ideas.
Best Sports Article Demonstrate outstanding quality of writing, analysis, and reporting.
Best Arts Reporting Demonstrate outstanding quality of writing and reporting.
Best Arts Review Demonstrate outstanding quality of writing and analysis.
Best Opinion Article Demonstrate outstanding quality of writing; make a clear, skillful, and reasonable argument.
Best Opinion Columnist Consistently demonstrate outstanding quality of writing over multiple pieces in a single column; make clear, skillful, and reasonable arguments.
Best Staff Editorials Consistently demonstrate clear, concise, and accessible arguments made as an organized editorial board; submissions that relate to school and local issues and do so effectively will be especially strong.
Best News Photographer Consistently demonstrate outstanding quality of photography and image quality; supplement and complement accompanying news articles.
Best Sports Photographer Consistently demonstrate outstanding quality of photography and image quality; supplement and complement accompanying sports articles.
Best Illustration Demonstrate outstanding artistic quality; clearly present information or an opinion.
Best Informational Graphic Demonstrate outstanding design quality; comprehensively and clearly communicate information.
